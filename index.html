Cristiano Ronaldo

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and the Portugal national team.

He is considered one of the greatest football players of all time, with numerous awards and records to his name.

Skills

  • Exceptional speed and agility
  • Precision shooting ability
  • Dribbling skills
  • Physical strength
  • Free-kick expertise

Accomplishments

  • 5-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • 4-time UEFA Champions League winner
  • European Golden Shoe recipient
  • Overall top scorer in both the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA European Championship
